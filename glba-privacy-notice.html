Privacy Principles

Leppla Capital Management respects the privacy of clients and prospective clients. We limit the collection and use of nonpublic personal information to what is reasonably needed to provide advisory services and communicate with you.

Information Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers, custodians, legal or tax professionals, regulators, or other parties as permitted or required by law and as needed to support client relationships.

Safeguards

We maintain policies and procedures designed to protect nonpublic personal information and restrict access to personnel and service providers with a legitimate business need.

Updates

This notice may be updated periodically to reflect changes in services, laws, or firm practices.
