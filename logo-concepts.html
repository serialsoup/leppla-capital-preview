Leppla Capital Management

Logo concept sheet

Ten directions that keep the current navy and gold identity, explore a more ownable mark, and avoid anything that feels like a pyramid scheme. The strongest routes suggest foundations, stewardship, clarity, and long-term ascent without leaning on a literal stack.

Best overall 01 Keystone Summit

Still connects to the pyramid idea, but the open keystone shape feels architectural and stable.

Safest 07 Open Ledger

Very advisory-friendly: transparent, fiduciary, and less likely to trigger the wrong association.

Most distinctive 08 Interlock Seal

Creates a compact monogram that could work well on social icons, documents, and signage.
