Investment Process

How to build a strong investment policy statement.

A clear investment policy can make decisions easier when markets move.

Five-minute readInvestment ProcessResources

An investment policy statement gives decision makers a shared framework before markets become emotional. It defines goals, constraints, risk limits, roles, and the process for review.

The best policy is written before the difficult decision arrives.

For organizations and fiduciaries, the policy is also a communication tool: it helps explain why decisions were made and how future changes should be evaluated.

01

Purpose

Clarify what the portfolio exists to support and who is responsible for decisions.

02

Limits

Document risk, liquidity, constraints, and review guidelines before stress arrives.

03

Cadence

Set a rhythm for reporting, review, and changes to the policy itself.
