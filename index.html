Our Principles

Clients are our only priority.

At Leppla Capital, clients are our only priority. We built this firm on three principles: Discipline, Integrity, and Results. Our fixed-fee management model is designed to ensure that every decision is made to maximize your long-term returns. As your assets grow, our fees stay the same.

Our Approach

The Securities Investment Pyramid™

Our Securities Investment Pyramid™ is read from the bottom up. Foundational investments form the base, alpha-oriented strategies sit above that foundation, and direct investments belong only near the top of the risk spectrum.

As an investor’s risk tolerance increases, the portfolio can expand upward. The relative percentages in each layer change by client profile, but the discipline stays the same: determine the base first, then decide how much risk belongs in the upper layers.

Fixed-Fee Management

Clients should keep more of what their portfolios earn.

Traditional variable management fees rise as assets grow, which directly reduces the benefits of compounding over time. At Leppla Capital Management, we charge a simple fixed fee of 50 basis points.

This rate is locked in for two years and is only reassessed at the end of each period. Our clients enjoy both lower overall costs and fee predictability, allowing their investments to compound more efficiently without the drag of escalating fees.
