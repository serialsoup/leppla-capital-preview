Our Approach

The Securities Investment Pyramid™

Our Securities Investment Pyramid™ redefines traditional portfolio construction by layering investments according to risk, with higher-risk assets positioned toward the apex. What sets our approach apart is its dynamic adaptability within a systematic framework.

As an investor’s risk tolerance increases, the pyramid expands upward from foundational investments through direct investments, while the relative percentages and specific holdings within each layer are adjusted. Every portfolio starts with a foundational fixed income base. While U.S. Treasuries may be appropriate for a conservative portfolio, an aggressive portfolio may prefer higher-grade corporate bonds.

Fixed-Fee Management

Clients should keep more of what their portfolios earn.

Traditional variable management fees rise as assets grow, which directly reduces the benefits of compounding over time. At Leppla Capital Management, we charge a simple fixed fee of 50 basis points.

This rate is locked in for two years and is only reassessed at the end of each period. Our clients enjoy both lower overall costs and fee predictability, allowing their investments to compound more efficiently without the drag of escalating fees.
